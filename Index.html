About our store

We propogate, grow, and sell Golden Ivy. It is a nice plant that stay green all year long, it needs minumum care and can survive almost in all environments.

The plant has a multitude of common names. It is also called devil's vine or devil's ivy because it is almost impossible to kill and it stays green even when kept in the dark. They're resistant to insects and disease, and are fast growing and vigorous.
